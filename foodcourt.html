Food Court Bakery

Get to know us

The Food Court Bakery is a proudly South African owned brand that produces the finest quality of baked products, from bread and rolls to confectionary. The Katlehong based company was initially establish as a fast food road house by Siyabonga Mahlobo in 2017, serving one of the best cooked meals, dagwood and kota around the neighbourhood -
hence name Food Court. As economic needs changed, so did the structure of the company.
